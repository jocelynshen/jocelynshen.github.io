JOCELYN SHEN

Hi! I'm Jocelyn, a junior at MIT pursuing a major in Computer Science alongisde a minor in Economics. I'm passionate about technology, behavioral science, and AI policy and ethics. The driving motivation behind my research and my projects is to understand the relationship between machines, individuals, and society. In the past, I've worked at Citadel (Software Engineering Intern), Facebook (Software Engineering Intern), and Affectiva (Data Science Intern). I'm also a researcher at the MIT Media Lab's Personal Robotics group, where I've worked on affective-computing and educational technology projects for the past three years. I hope to use my knowledge in CS and machine learning to better understand human behavior and contribute to causes related to mental disability, education, and assistive technology, as well as drive research behind policy decisions regarding artificial intelligence and equitable technology. Aside from my work, I'm a writer and digital artist based in Los Angeles.
